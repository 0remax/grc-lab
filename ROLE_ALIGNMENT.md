# GRC Role Alignment
I have structured my technical expertise to align with core Governance, Risk, and Compliance functions. Below is how my skills map to industry-standard roles:

## How My Portfolio Supports GRC Roles

* **Third-Party Risk Analyst / Officer**
    * **My Capability:** I utilize my [Vendor Risk Assessment](THIRD_PARTY_VENDOR_RISK.md) module to vet vendors, evaluate security postures, and identify supply chain vulnerabilities.
* **Vendor Risk Analyst / Consultant**
    * **My Capability:** I am proficient in reviewing external certifications, issuing security questionnaires, and defining data access risk boundaries for third-party partnerships.
* **IT Compliance Officer / Specialist**
    * **My Capability:** I conduct internal audits against international standards such as ISO 27001, ITIL, and NIST cybersecurity frameworks.
* **Information Security Analyst**
    * **My Capability:** I proactively interpret security logs, manage system patch deployments, and evaluate corporate access permissions to eliminate privilege creep.
* **IT Assurance Analyst**
    * **My Capability:** I document changes in system configurations, author highly structured technical Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), and prepare auditable reports for executive review.
